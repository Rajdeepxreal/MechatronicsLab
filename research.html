Research Areas

AI & Machine Learning

Developing intelligent robots that can learn, adapt, and make decisions in complex environments. We explore deep learning, reinforcement learning, and computer vision.

Human-Robot Interaction

Designing intuitive and safe ways for humans to collaborate with robots. Our work includes gesture control, natural language interfaces, and collaborative robotics (cobots).

Autonomous Systems

Creating self-driving vehicles, drones, and mobile robots that can navigate and operate autonomously using advanced sensor fusion and path-planning algorithms.

Medical Robotics

Engineering robotic systems for surgical assistance, rehabilitation, and patient care to improve healthcare outcomes and accessibility.

Advanced Manufacturing

Implementing robotics and automation to create smart factories, focusing on precision assembly, quality control, and flexible manufacturing systems.

Agricultural Robotics

Developing automated solutions for farming, including robotic harvesting, crop monitoring, and precision agriculture to enhance food production sustainably.
